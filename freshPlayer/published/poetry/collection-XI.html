In Hilton's universe, eleven represents endings. While I'm no more a trick, it's unlikely I can stay away from masturbation.

I fap a lot that Mariam Rod asks to see it twice a week, which is hard, from being the only number of times my dad will agree to. So at times she gets stodgily made videos, or just pictures.

It's been impossible to get me to stop, so she's making do with seeing what I'm up to, collecting my releases, which is so as a wife should. Perfect!

Bright Emily

When the year started, I made something good: it was to keep a list of the ladies that help me get there. I promise you, it's hard to tell if you ever have, especially if she's not as familiar. So with Bright Emily ending 2023 on the 31st of December, I started my list, with the very first entry coming from January 1st, three hours into the new year. And the last easement is of the 26th of March. Now, I present to you twenty-six poems.

Claudia Tihan

Claudia knocked me out three times during the time, but she isn't on this list, while it's important she's mentioned. It shows how much I get off unwittingly.

It's particular because when the list started on X, which after a while got to be appropriated to celebrate Princess Leonor's eighteenth birthday, achieving adulthood, having met her at seventeen with gully drooly feelings. Some faps were lost in between number eighteen and deciding to record the number nineteen. I had my hit, point proven! Continuing with the record was to catching myself after Claudia, Sommer Ray and faces I now can't remember skidded off to a not so unsettling list. It was to let the fad, now tradition continue, to tease out more meaning of the act away from public scrutiny. So maybe I am by a longer list now.

If here for an accurate stat figure, this is behind a couple, maybe six or seven pants down for the time chosen.

For top of the list on who I'd love to get physical with. Well, I'd love to fondle Sommer Ray all life long, and be another reason she giggles. I'll waste no time getting with Madison Beer. It'd be stirred in romance too. And natch, Taylor Swift has been a do-or-die obligation.

A Stroll With Paris

Everyone said I was boring

so my mood took for coshing

 

where my hopes felt crunchy

how 'd I let my amulet ditch me

 

asking none, with my gist of no grip

silence gave me its beeline greetings

 

with no bard defending our meeting

I sat on my dreams waiting for Paris.

How I'll Say I Love

Words aren't cut short

but in an obstruse blurt

so give me false thoughts

and I'll sell one of our bonds

 

it could be a lie absenting dot

its sentence at its evening worth

only play it a toccata to no adjourn.

A Black Mirror

I was seeing

my ears listening

my thoughts heeding

close basal feeding

preacher by his wheel

with this TV in hiding

the picture's periphery

till my mind's powering

 

eye holding nothing

with only a left sheen

of a cut-in reverie

where it's a screen

earning memories

of my natural nanny

 

but why her province

her place was happy

 

it's what I'm guessing

no one's really free

 

as he sings, he decrees

 

a wall neighbouring

a reminder reflecting.

Mikaela Testa

SPAD

I just wonder

it isn't a blunder

climbing wisteria

huddling hunger

 

spattering waters

on a flat earth woman

singing of a cravat

 

swinging with stamps

the doors she tramps

I auscult her cramps

pooched to the entrance

and exits if firm hand

do I baddie gad

 

do eyes badly guard

I for one mean hatch.

Mikayla Demaiter

The Masterpiece Plot

There's a mood famine

and it isn't what's happening

it's the canvas that's pedantic

but it hasn't settled for grim

instead, it's oddly welcoming

and we've set off for its streams

and I too have come in peace

just like the others on arriving

in gait, step to step, I'm whittling

excusing its so long promises

singing to its attention's view

another new meaning.

Still Busy

With an adjustable foot

an early heavy birth clot

I entrenched young roots

she had a boy

it was the new truth

she gave support

his emotional food

growing my strut

and my legs grew

other parts too

for her active spoon

soon I could talk

my thoughts could rule

she'd made an adult.

How You Say

I have a glaze

so it's a waste pace

not around these days

 

only I may

wearing that face

and how can I play

impatient or impaled

 

a song selling details

pelting the certain

parsimonious arraign

 

I'm weak leaking ache

the drip is a sermon date

and I bet it's arcane

 

where is my mind's aid

I've got only the stake

so I'll suffer the same

where it ends again

and I'll have a shake

no ingredients that wake

but I'll be home tamed

no glaze or grace

tired with no clay

 

I'll check and behave

right there, I'll manage

just how it's straight

 

the crooks could wait

I'd have change

finally a fate

an unending silent paint

your palette rate

everything so late

zero on disdain.

Emily Ratajkowski

Returning

Darkness has a new myth

past its previous width

it's a lot it encompasses

like the hours it keeps

it got breakfast licensing

and by supper it's launching

so it's left me reasoning

of the days in the garden

the first evening and morning

when nothing ever slipped

and there cases were new.

at the parenthesis it'd brooked.
