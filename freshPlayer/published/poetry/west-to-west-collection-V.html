Dedication

Tate McRae

Foreword

Tate Mcrae.

Shallots Picking

All stairs bore me

like insects not stinging

beauty incense captivating

inducing some of its siblings

tofu mirth, the giddying yeast

I know, we're going, but a brim?

Idols

I swear, they have seats named

same ones missing in soccer games

I'm here for you all of the 365 days

pop music playing the best of the greats.

Same Old Rafters

My head is up to the stars

their eyes send me in a dither

the twinkling of my own ever after.

Brunch

Double sheets of the cheese

a slice of onion on margarine

she was all I had imagined.
